About Us

 We're your source for OpenSolaris in the Toronto and GTA. Monthly meetings, special events and outreach to the Toronto Open Source community is our mandate.
 Sign up to our mail-lists and come on out to our meetings, you're sure to have a blast! Suggestions & comments are most welcome!
 We had our first meeting in conjunction with Unix Unanimous (what a great bunch they are), at the Bahen Centre Computer Science, University of Toronto on August 12, 2009. Among other topics we discussed zfs recovery, atheros wifi drivers and tracing problems. After the meeting the majority of us trekked to a local Thai restaurant for some good eats and cold beer, continuing the discussions. A very cool night. I invite all to join up .. we'll get some install-fests going, some guest speakers and of course some SWAG. Between all that we'll learn, experiment and improve. Keep an eye on our mail-lists and this space for meeting dates!
