Adelaide

Purpose

The Adelaide (Open)Solaris User Group (ADL-AUOSUG) seeks to drive (Open)Solaris awareness and adoption amongst the enthusiast and industry community in Adelaide, Australia.

The target audience is initially UNIX enthusiasts who use UNIX at home or work. We perceive that a significant number of UNIX enthusiasts in the area will be interested in meeting and exchanging ideas about OpenSolaris and related technologies. We want to contribute documentation and small code changes back to the community. Once established we could broaden scope to include students and would encourage adoption of OpenSolaris in student projects.

Our first meeting was part of a Sun Microsystems Technical Update on Tuesday 19th February 2007.

Outputs

The project will archive its contributions, presentations, and other material to the project space on the OpenSolaris site.

Contacting

  • Join the User Group mailing list: http://mail.opensolaris.org/mailman/listinfo/ug-adl-osug and contact the User Group via the mailing list: ug dash adl dash osug at opensolaris dot org 
  • Phone one of the leaders, Alex Sims on 0458 67 0011

Activities

  • Technical meetings (Generally 4:30-6:00pm, the fourth Tuesday of each month excluding December at Oracle, Level 19, 25 Grenfell St, Adelaide) Google Calendar of all OSUGs.
  • Discussions on our mailing list
