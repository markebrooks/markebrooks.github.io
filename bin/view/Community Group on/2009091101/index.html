HEADS UP: Marvell Yukon 2 Gigabit Ethernet support added

HEADS UP: Marvell Yukon 2 Gigabit Ethernet support added


Date: Fri, 11 Sep 2009 20:30:44 -0700
From: barry harding <Barry.Harding at Sun dot COM>
To: onnv-gate at onnv dot eng dot sun dot com
Subject: HEADS UP: Marvell Yukon 2 Gigabit Ethernet support added

                If you do not have a system with a "Marvell Yukon 2 Gigabit Ethernet"
                chip you can ignore this message.

                The push of "6819638 Marvell Yukon 2 Ethernet controller driver not found"
                (PSARC 2009/190) integrates support for the yge driver on x86 and amd64
                systems. This driver will support Sparc when "6875654 yge should be
                supported on SPARC" integrates.

                This driver is known to work with the following PCI ID's:

                11ab,4354 - 88E8040
                11ab,4362 - 88E8053
                11ab,4363 - 88E8055
                11ab,4364 - 88E8056
                11ab,436a - 88E8058
                1186,4b00 - DGE-560T (D-Link)

                While the Marvell chips have a large set of legacy devices in addition to the
                ones listed above. These are the only chip types that have been tested and are
                known to work at this time.

                - Barry
