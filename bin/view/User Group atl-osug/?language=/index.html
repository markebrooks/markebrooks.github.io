Charter

The Atlanta OpenSolaris User Group (ATLOSUG) has been established to connect members of the community interested in OpenSolaris and Solaris, and to grow the OpenSolaris community in the Atlanta area.  All are welcome - developers, open-source contributors, system administrator, architects, and any other interested parties.

Meetings

ATLOSUG holds its meetings on the 2nd Tuesday of each month.  Meetings are almost always held at GCA Technology Services off Northridge Rd in Sandy Springs, but we also occasionally meet at the Oracle office in Alpharetta.  Meetings run from 7:00 to 9:00PM, with networking and light refreshments beginning at 6:30PM.
