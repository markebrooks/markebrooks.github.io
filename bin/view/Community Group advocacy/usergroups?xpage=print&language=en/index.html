User Group Background

Welcome to User Groups

The OpenSolaris User Groups (OSUGs) are sponsored by the Advocacy Community on opensolaris.org, and all the information you need to get involved is right here.

  • Join the Advocacy Community
  • Join an OpenSolaris User Group
  • Start a New OpenSolaris User Group
  • OSUG Leader Responsibilities on opensolaris.org
  • Help, Contacts, FAQs

Join the Advocacy Community

The first step in getting involved in OpenSolaris User Groups on opensolaris.org is to join the Advocacy Community.

Join an OpenSolaris User Group

Look for user groups in your area. If you find one, simply join that group's list, introduce yourself, get to know the members, and participate. If you find a group that used to exist but is no longer active, send mail to osug-leaders and/or advocacy-discuss to determine if the group can be restarted. Here are 10,000 images of OpenSolaris people around the world to help you associate names and faces.

Start a New OpenSolaris User Group

Community Groups, Projects, and User Groups are approved via processes outlined in the website infrastructure procedures document.

OSUG Leader Responsibilities on opensolaris.org

As a leader of your new user group, it is expected that you will maintain your XWiki space and Mailman mailing list (filter spam, add/remove names, communicate with the OpenSolaris webmaster and other community members on advocacy-discuss and osug-leaders, etc), just as all the other Collective Leaders in the OpenSolaris Community. Please review the documents below so you are clear about website issues, especially the policies around mail list management. For instance, if lists are not used and properly managed, they can be deleted after a period of time.

Follow the appropriate website, trademark, project, and community policies:

  • OpenSolaris Website Community
  • Website User Guide
  • OpenSolaris Website Guidelines
  • OpenSolaris Trademarks Policy
  • Website Roles & Collectives
  • Infrastructure Life Cycle Instructions
  • OpenSolaris Mailing List Policy
