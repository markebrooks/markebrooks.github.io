Oklahoma City

About Us

The Oklahoma City OpenSolaris User Group brings together students, sysadmins, developers, engineers, IT managers and folks in the metro area and surroundings areas interested in discovering more about OpenSolaris and Sun Solaris operating systems.

We welcome you, and encourage you to join us.

Please Join Email Discussion List

Particpate and let your voice be heard as we develop our community together.

The email alias for the Oklahoma City OpenSolaris User Group is hosted at OpenSolaris.

To subscribe, visit the mail list administration site  ug-okcosug .

Announcements and news will be sent to this list.

Meetings

The Oklahoma City OpenSolaris User's group (OKCOSUG) next meeting will be on Thursday, April 8th at Oklahoma City University. John Robertson, Unix Sr Admin and OKCOSUG Member, will be the featured speaker. The meeting will run from 5:30 to 7:30PM, with light refreshments beginning at 5:00PM.
 
Agenda 
 5:00PM to 5:30PM Meet and Greet
 5:30PM to 7:00PM Solaris zones and Containers Technical Overview, Demonstration, Further Resources
 Oracle Virtual Machine Overview (Yes, you can install OpenSolaris as a guest in an Oracle VM for x86 environment)
 7:00PM to 7:15PM Summary and open for questions 

About our Speaker: 

John Robertson has been working with Solaris systems since 1996 (back in the  solaris 2.5.1 days). He's worked on hardware from a Sparc Center 2000 all  the way to the M4000s. Not only has he worked with the OS, but he's worked on oracle databases, application servers, and IT governance. 

Sponsors: 
Oklahoma City Universtity and Lumenate

Directions to Oklahoma City University

Oklahoma City University is located on NW 23rd and Blackwelder. The address is 2501 North Blackwelder, Oklahoma City, Ok 73106. However, we are meeting in the Auditorium room of The Meinders School of Business at Oklahoma City University. Click Here to see The School Campus Map. The Meinders School of Business is building 630. 
 Google map OCU Meinders School of Business Here

From the north via I-35

Getting to Oklahoma City University from the north via I-35: From Wichita and points north, take I-35 south to the I-44 junction (immediate communities north of Oklahoma City are Guthrie and Edmond). Take I-44 west (right lanes), to the I-235 (Centennial Expressway) interchange. Exit (right) and take I-235 south to downtown (this requires driving under the overpass and taking a cloverleaf up to the right so you are heading southward on the overpass under which you just drove). Continue south to N.W. 23rd Street and exit (right); at the traffic light at the bottom of the exit ramp, turn west (right) toward the university. Take 23rd to McKinley Avenue (past Robinson, Walker, Shartel, Western, Classen, Ellison, Douglass and Brauer ). McKinley is the next street . At McKinley, turn north on to campus. See OCU Campus map link Here. We are meeting on Building 630 Meinders School of Business Kerr-McGee Auditorium.
