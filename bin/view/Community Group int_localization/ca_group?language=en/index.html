Catalan Group

Catalan localization group at OpenSolaris

This is the Catalan community page of OpenSolaris users, that performs localization tasks at OpenSolaris.

What is OpenSolaris?

OpenSolaris has various meanings depending on witch content is used, for example, OpenSolaris is:

  • An operative system initially based on SolarisTM operative system code, Sun's property.
  • An open source development project.
