Promise SuperTrak RAID HBA Driver

Promise SuperTrak RAID HBA Driver Project

The purpose of this project is to provide early access to a Solaris driver that will enable RAID on OpenSolaris systems with Promise SATA and SAS/SATA RAID PCI-express controller hardware.

The sts driver ("SuperTrak Storage" HBA) belongs to the SATA/SAS line of Host Bus Adapters that provide hardware RAID storage. The sts driver was a jointly developed CDDL driver based on BSD source version by Promise. Sun paid for contract work to produce the driver under CDDL by Dexcel Corp in India. This line of cards features Intel I/O processors for SATA-only and SAS/SATA (aka Brockton and Sunrise Lake).

Promise Technology, Inc. is a storage HBA vendor that provides both small business to large enterprise storage HBAs and finished Storage Arrays. Promise currently provides Solaris-Ready certified VTrak storage subsystems.
