  • Callout / Timeout Re-implementation
    Status: complete.
     Integrated into snv_103, tracked by CRs 6565503 and [[6311743 >>http://bugs.opensolaris.org/bugdatabase/view_bug.do?bug_id=6565503]]
  • clock() decoupled lbolt / lbolt64

  • Tick processing, Tick accounting

Open Development

The Tickless Architecture project is developed in the open and welcomes anyones interested in participating, either through code, documentation or simply in dicussions. This happens in our development alias

tickless-dev@opensolaris.org

 which you can subscribe(/unsubscribe) by sending an empty email to

tickless-dev-subscribe@opensolaris.org

The participants can also be found on the #pm-dev channel on irc.freenode.net
