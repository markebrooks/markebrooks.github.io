Munich OpenSolaris User Group

Welcome to the Munich Open Solaris User Group!

If you are near Munich, Germany and love OpenSolaris, then this group is just for you.

Join us now by subscribing to our discussion list or check out our Meetings and list of Files & Links.

Spread the word and tell your OpenSolaris loving friends in Munich about this group. This page is easy to remember and easy to reach: Just type mucosug.de into your browser.
