Arizona


Welcome to the Arizona OpenSolaris User Group (AZOSUG), located in Phoenix, Arizona. Our purpose is to provide a method by which users of OpenSolaris and Solaris, as well as Sun hardware and software can come together to exchange ideas, learn about new technologies and social networking.
