OpenSolaris 社区链接

 This page points to links within the greater OpenSolaris community. It's just a quick glance at a wide variety of resources available to the community. The goal is to grow the page so the community has a convenient place find additional resources. Some of these links appear on opensolaris.org or on Sun's various sites, but we'd also like to point to additional resources. Please send suggestions to website-discuss.

Solaris Systems Programming by Rich Teer (Chinese translation here)
OpenSolaris by Rolf Dietze, Tatjana Heusser, Jörg Schilling

Project Looking Glass Development community for Project Looking Glass.
