10/2008 Meeting

New York {Open}Solaris User Group - NYCOSUG The 12th New York {Open}Solaris Community User Group meeting is here! 

 The New York OpenSolaris User's Group (NYCOSUG) will be holding it's next monthly meeting on Thursday, October 30th at the offices of Sun Microsystems Inc., in midtown New York City.

 [Please RSVP by 10/28, so we have an approximate count]

 We'll get started with refreshments @ 6pm.

 We will have two topics at this event:
 1) Introduction to Puppet - what it is and how can it make system administration less painful.
 See www.reductivelabs.com
 Speaker - Larry Ludwig - Principal Consultant/Founder of Empowering Media. Empowering Media is a consulting firm and managed hosting provider.
Larry Ludwig has been in the industry for over 15 years as a system administration and system programmer. He's had previous experience working for many Fortune 500 corporations and holds a BS in CS from Clemson University.
 Larry, along with Eric Moore and Brian Gupta are founding members of the NYC Puppet usergroup.
 2) OpenSolaris in Storage - trends in the storage industry and how OpenSolaris is shaping directions. Speaker - Neal Weiss - Global Financial Services Architect, Sun Microsystems Inc.
 Neal Weiss is a senior member of the NY Sun technical engineering community, with over 20 years experience in the storage industry.

 Meeting will start at 6pm with light refreshments, etc.
