Chinese Users

Welcome to the Chinese Users Community

 This community serves as a portal for Chinese users. The mail list of this community is zh-users-discuss at OpenSolaris dot org. You can subscribe to it by sending a blank email to zh-users-discuss-request at OpenSolaris dot org or visit the link. You can also post messages through online forum. Currently, there are two sub-communities which are targeting Chinese users specifically:

  • OpenSolaris Marketing of China
     This sub-community is started by Siyuan Tian and is interested in the following topics:
    • Successful cases of using (Open)Solaris in Chinese industries, universities and institutes, hope to develop some model from here
    • What new functions/features users would like to have in (Open)Solaris
  • User Groups of China
     This sub-community is started by Victor Hu and is interested in creating a platform for Chinese users to learn, communicate, participate and collaborate. Local user groups will be created as needed in regions of China. Here is the list of user groups in China:
    • Beijing OpenSolaris User Group
       The mail list of this user group is ug-bjosug at OpenSolaris dot org. You can subscribe to it by sending a blank email to ug-bjosug-request at OpenSolaris dot org or visit the link. You can also post messages through online forum.
    •  Shanghai OpenSolaris User Group
  • 简体中文工作组
     负责OpenSolaris的简体中文本地化以及相关国际化方面的工作。 this is a test edit.
