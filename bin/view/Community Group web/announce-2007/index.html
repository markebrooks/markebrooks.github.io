Announcements Archive: 2007

These are the announcements on opensolaris.org from 2007. After 2007, the announcements feature was no longer used, and in Oct of 2009 the section was deleted. The content is archived here in the Website Community. OpenSolaris announcements are sent to the opensolaris-announce mailing list and do not appear on the site.

February 2007

OpenSolaris Starter Kit | 02/28/2007

Global distribution of the new OpenSolaris Starter Kit is now live at http://get.opensolaris.org/. There is also a link at http://www.opensolaris.org/os/. The kit contains tutorials, docs, community distributions, source code, and tools all delivered on two DVDs for free.

Japan OpenSolaris Portal Opens | 02/22/2007

The Japanese OpenSolaris community opened their portal today: http://jp.opensolaris.org/

This represents the first country portal on opensolaris.org. The project is just beginning, though, so if you'd like to participate in the development of the Japan OpenSolaris portal, sign up on the discussions page here http://jp.opensolaris.org/forums/. The announcement from Sun Japan is here http://jp.sun.com/company/Press/release/2007/0222.html.

The Japan portal is part of the OpenSolaris country portal project, which is an effort to translate the content on opensolaris.org into as many languages as possible: http://opensolaris.org/os/project/portals/. It's a community effort, so you are more than welcome to get involved: http://opensolaris.org/os/project/portals/discussions/.

Jim Grisanzio, Community Manager, OpenSolaris
