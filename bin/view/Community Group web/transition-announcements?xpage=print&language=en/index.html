Website Transition: Announcements

The documents linked below contain announcements from the restructuring activities for opensolaris.org: plan development, source and binary releases, testing, open evaluation processes, conference calls, implementation details, and updates. Many more discussions about the website transition took place on the lists since 2007, but these are the major announcements for the project. Where links do not appear, either documents have been updated/replaced or the communication took place on a venue that does not offer links (either direct email to users or to a private list). In those cases, we merely document that the communication took place for transparency reasons, but please note that nothing confidential was discussed in those communications.

  1. Updates to bugs.opensolaris.org 11/11/09
    New version of bugs.opensolaris.org deployed.
  2. Moratorium on new Collectives Lifted 10/28/09
    Began setting up new Collectives on the new site. See Collective Life Cycle Instructions here.
  3. New OpenSolaris Website is Live 10/26/09
    opensolaris.org moves to XWiki on markebrooks.github.io.
  4. Schedule for Final Website Transition on Mon Oct 2 10/23/09
    Details for the final content migration to XWiki on Monday October 26th. This announcement ends the Phase 2 interim period, which consisted of 31 content migrations over 3 months to enable the community to prepare content for the final move to XWiki.
  5. Website Transition Update: 1 Week Left 10/20/09
    29th content migration in 11 weeks. One week warning.
  6. Auth account validation deadline 10/14/09
    Personal email communication to opensolaris.org users who have not validated their accounts under the new Auth system.
  7. Website Transition Update: 2 Weeks Left 10/13/09
    26th content migration & 2 weeks left to the final content move on 10/26/09.
  8. opensolaris.org website changes 10/08/09
    Personal email communication to all users on opensolaris.org about the final date of 10/26/09 to move to XWiki.
  9. Website Transition Update: 3 Weeks Left 10/5/09
    23rd content migration & 3 weeks left to the final content move on 10/26/09.
  10. Auth Updated 10/5/09
    CGs role names, Electorates
  11. Scheduled Downtime for Auth System Update 10/1/09
    Change the roles in CGs to accommodate the OGB and add access to CG Electorates for the OGB Secretary.

    The switch to XWiki, which has been scheduled for Sept 14th, will be delayed approximately 2-3 weeks to complete new work on the roles in CGs and to do additional testing. New final date for the Phase 2 transition to XWiki is TBD.

All migrations will be posted to website-discuss and/or opensolaris-announce in Aug/Sept but the mails will not be listed here as well. Also, many of these content migration emails have been sent to all the OSUGs (about 150 lists) and all the key Projects and Community Groups (well over 100 lists).
