Taiwan OpenSolaris User Group

社群簡介

歡迎您來到台灣的 OpenSolaris 愛用者群組(TWOSUG)首頁! 這個空間是提供給所有對開放原始碼作業系統有高度熱忱的您，假如您曾經在工作上或家裡使用過任何開放原始碼的軟體，或者是您想要更深入地了解 OpenSolaris 這個開源的作業系統，我們都歡迎您加入我們的行列。

我們不定期會在台灣各地舉辦愛用者聚會，每次聚會的時程與議程細節都會公布在此頁以及左邊方框的 活動訊息 連結。 

郵遞論壇與討論區

  • 您可以在 這裡 訂閱我們的郵遞論壇。
  • 您也可以張貼您的各種問題與意見到我們的 討論區。

台灣 OpenSolaris 愛用者社群大會 (98年8月14日)

這一次的社群大會我們很榮幸的邀請到兩位講者(Jim Grisanzio & Eric Li), Jim 來自於 Sun Japan 分公司, 一直以來都是 OpenSolaris.org 社群中的核心人物, 這次他將與我們分享一下 OpenSolaris.org 社群的前世今生以及對未來的規劃; Eric 是 Sun Taiwan 相當資深的技術經理, 他應該是在台灣這邊使用 OpenSolaris 當作日常 desktop 最有歷史也是將之應用地最淋漓盡致的人, 所以這次很榮幸的請他撥空來這邊跟我們分享一下 "Good Uses on OpenSolaris Desktop"。

會中我們還會發送 OpenSolaris 2009.06 的 LiveCD，您可以攜帶自己的筆電或隨身碟(2GB+)來馬上體驗或安裝。

此次聚會的詳細資訊如下：

  • 主題： OpenSolaris.org 社群介紹與演示最創新的 OS 
  • 時間： 8 月 14 日 6:30pm - 9pm
  • 地點： 台灣昇陽電腦 (台北市信義區松高路11號11樓)
  • 點心： 將提供點心與飲料
  • 議程:
    • 6:30pm - 7:00pm: 報到與享用點心
    • 7:00pm - 8:00pm: "Contributing to the OpenSolaris Project" by Jim Grisanzio
    • 8:00pm - 9:00pm: "Good Uses on OpenSolaris Desktop" by Eric Li
  • 講者的部落格:
    • Jim Grisanzio (http://blogs.sun.com/jimgris/)
    • Eric Li (http://blog.xuite.net/evereasy/solarpower)
  • 專業能力： 此次聚會不限任何專業能力的人都能參加，只要您對開放原始碼的軟體有興趣與熱情，我們都很歡迎您的蒞臨。

注意： 如欲參加者，請於 8 月 13 日前寄一封郵件到 <ug-twosug@opensolaris.org>，郵件主旨請註明[參加 OpenSolaris 愛用者社群大會]，郵件內文請註明您的公司與大名!
請立即報名，座位有限!

請與會者到達後於一樓觸控螢幕處通知我們以領取通行證，刷卡通過右邊的關卡後直接上 11 樓。

Taiwan OpenSolaris User Group Meeting (August 14th, 2009)

It's with great pleasure to have 2 speakers (Jim Grisanzio & Eric Li) with us at this meeting. Jim is from Sun Japan office and he has taken several major roles within OpenSolaris.org community for the past few years. He will share with us the community building efforts of OpenSolaris.org, the scope of its history and the future planning, etc. Eric is from Sun Taiwan and serves as a Senior Technical Manager role. He is no doubt the most experienced OpenSolaris desktop users here in Taiwan so we are so glad to have him here to demonstrate all the good uses on OpenSolaris desktop. 

We will also distribute OpenSolaris 2009.06 LiveCDs for you to get a hands-on experience of this evolutionary OS and answer all questions you may have. If you want to have a bootable LiveUSB of osol0906, please remember to bring USB stick with you (2GB+).

The meeting details are as follows:

  • Topic: OpenSolaris.org Community Intro & The most innovative desktop OS demo
  • Date/Time: August 14th, 2009 6:30pm-9pm
  • Location: Sun Taiwan Office (11F, 11 SongGao Road, Taipei)
  • Refreshments: Light food and drinks will be provided
  • Agenda:
    • 6:30pm - 7:00pm: Registration and Refreshments
    • 7:00pm - 8:00pm: "Contributing to the OpenSolaris Project" by Jim Grisanzio
    • 8:00pm - 9:00pm: "Good Uses on OpenSolaris Desktop" by Eric Li
  • Speakers' Blog:
    • Jim Grisanzio (http://blogs.sun.com/jimgris/)
    • Eric Li (http://blog.xuite.net/evereasy/solarpower)
  • Experience: All levels of experience are welcome. If you are enthusiastic with open source software, we are more than happy to see you here.

Attention: Please RSVP to <ug-twosug@opensolaris.org> before August 13th. Looking forward to meeting you!
Seats are limited, please RSVP NOW!

Note: All attendees please register at downstairs kiosk to get the building pass. After validating at the security gate, please come directly to 11th Floor.
