Looking to see if someone has already converted a service you use to smf(5)? Want to save your peers time, or impress us all with your novel use of the Service Management Facility? Find or submit the service conversion to smf-discuss AT opensolaris.org, and it will appear here. If possible, simply send a link to the manifest, and give it a copyright and (OSI-approved) license.
